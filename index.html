What is microfrontends?

Microfrontend is the splitting of a monolithic application into semantically isolated parts and independent development of this parts.

Is it difficult to understand?

Let's try an example!

Example

Step 1

We have an app or website.

Step 2

On each page we have different blocks:

  • admin panel
  • catalog page
  • shopping cart
  • etc...

Step 3

Each of these blocks can be considered as a microfrontend unit, and this unit that can be independently:

  • developed
  • tested
  • deployed

Step 4

At the same time, the blocks must be able to interact with each other, cause they are part of the same application

Why was this technology created?

For a long time, the application was developed according to an architectural conception called "monolith".

Monolith had some disadvantages:

difficult to scale

difficult to develop

difficult to maintain

difficult to test

Microfronends advantages:

Solves the problems of monolithic architecture

Independent development teams can work on different parts of the project

You can use different frameworks in different microfrontend units
